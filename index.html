Who We Are

WeStudy is a student-centered tutoring service helping children from Junior Kindergarten through Grade 12 build confidence, improve understanding, and succeed academically.

Founded over 6 years ago, WeStudy has grown into a team of 10 university-level tutors who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students across all subjects.

We offer both in-person and virtual tutoring, tailoring each session to the student’s learning style. Our flexible scheduling and responsive communication make it easy for families to find what works best.

How Virtual Tutoring Works

  1. You’ll receive a Google Classroom invite link via email.
  2. Click the link and sign in with a Google account.
  3. Lesson materials and announcements are posted in Classroom.
  4. Google Meet links are shared inside Classroom for live sessions.
  5. Students join the session by clicking the Meet link at class time.
