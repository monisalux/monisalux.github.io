About Us

WeStudy is a student-focused tutoring service dedicated to helping children from Junior Kindergarten through Grade 12 succeed academically and build confidence.

Founded over 6 years ago, WeStudy has grown into a team of 10 university-level tutors who are passionate about education and mentoring students at every stage of their learning journey.

We offer support in all subjects and provide both in-person and virtual tutoring. Our approach is flexible and personalized — we adapt to each student’s learning style and work closely with families to create schedules that fit their needs.

Parents choose WeStudy for our flexible hours, responsive communication, and our commitment to making learning effective, supportive, and stress-free.

How Virtual Classes Work

  1. After registration, students will receive a Google Classroom invite link via email.
  2. Click the link and sign in using a Google account to join the class.
  3. All lesson materials, announcements, and assignments will be posted in Google Classroom.
  4. For virtual sessions, tutors will post a Google Meet link directly inside the Classroom.
  5. At the scheduled time, students simply click the Meet link to join the live session.

No additional software is required — everything runs through Google Classroom and Google Meet.
